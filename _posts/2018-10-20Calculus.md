### 四则运算
#### 乘
$$
(uv)' = uv'+v'u
$$
推导：
$$
\Delta(uv) = u(x+\Delta x)v(x+\Delta x) - u(x)v(x)
$$
$$
= (u(x+\Delta x)-u(x))v(x+\Delta x)+u(x)(v(x+\Delta x)-v(x))
$$
$$
=\Delta uv+u\Delta v
$$

$$
\frac{\text{d}(uv)}{\text{d}x} =  v\frac{\text {d} u}{\text{d}x}+u\frac{\text{d}v}{\text dx}
$$
#### 除
$$
(\frac{u}{v})' = \frac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}
$$
